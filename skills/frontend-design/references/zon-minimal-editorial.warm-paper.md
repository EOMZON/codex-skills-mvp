# Zon Minimal Editorial — Warm Paper

Use this when you want the same minimal editorial structure, but with a warm “paper” tone (less stark than pure black/white).

## Tokens

- `--black: #141311`
- `--white: #fbf7ef`
- `--gray-100: #f3ecdf`
- `--gray-200: #e6dccb`
- `--gray-400: #9a8f7f`
- `--gray-600: #6f665a`
- `--gray-800: #2a2722`
- Optional accent: `--accent: #8b6b3d`

## Notes

- Keep borders/rules slightly warmer (`--gray-200`).
- Highlight uses a warm wash: `rgba(139,107,61,0.14)` (subtle).
- Best for: essays, long notes, reflective interviews.
